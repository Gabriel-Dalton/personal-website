About me

I was born on a boat and grew up loving tech, design, and sustainability. Now I run Oasis of Change Inc., where I build fast, eco-friendly websites and help organizations make a real impact online.

I've helped plant nearly 9,000 trees, worked with nonprofits, churches, and small businesses, and even taught seniors how to use tech. Whether it's clean code or meaningful projects, I'm all about using what I know to create something good.
